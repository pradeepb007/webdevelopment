Dear Subscriber,
   
   
Who would have ever imagined, we could get to spend so much time with family.
It’s something we’ve always wanted to do but never got around to it. Reconnecting, enjoying their company, reminiscing old memories, the list goes on. Almost 60 days post lockdown, are we still thinking the same? A majority of us will resonate with the following: I need time alone, I need to get back to work, I need a break, I want to get out.
Family can be fun but can be extremely intrusive as well. I’ve curated articles that may be handy for couples and families living together. Hope you find them interesting & easy to implement as well.
Warm regards,
